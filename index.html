Short Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering Department in The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Before that, I received a BEng. degree and a M.S. degree from Xidian University and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2011 and 2014, respectively.

I am widely interested in, but not limited to

  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Virtual Reality/Augmented Reality
  • Computer Vision
  • Computer Graphics
